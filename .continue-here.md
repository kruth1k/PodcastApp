## Known Issue (Fixed)

**Navigation audio desync bug:**
- When user plays episode → navigates to stats → comes back to home
- Audio continues playing but player bar shows paused
- Clicking play causes 2 audio tracks to play (Audio 1 + Audio 2)
- Only Audio 2 can be controlled

**Root cause:** 
- `setLastPlayed` in page.tsx runs on EVERY page mount (useEffect with isAuthenticated)
- It sets `howlInstance: null` which destroys the reference to Audio 1
- Audio 1 keeps playing (orphaned) but UI loses control of it
- Clicking play creates a NEW Audio 2

**Fix applied:**
1. playerStore.ts: Added check in togglePlay for `!isPlaying` but `howlInstance.playing()` = true → sync state
2. page.tsx: Added guard in loadLastPlayed - skip if howlInstance exists and episode matches

**Status:** Fixed - refresh to test
