# PodcastStats

## What This Is

A podcast player application that prioritizes comprehensive statistics tracking, giving users deep insights into their listening habits including minutes watched/saved, most listened podcasts/episodes, and time-based analytics grouped by week, month, and year.

## Core Value

Users can view detailed listening statistics across all podcasts and episodes, with powerful filtering and grouping by time periods (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly).
